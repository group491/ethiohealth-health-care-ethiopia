About EthiHealth

EthiHealth is a digital health awareness platform created to provide reliable, easy-to-understand, and locally relevant medical information for all Ethiopians. Our mission is to make accurate health education available to everyone, especially those living in rural and underserved areas.

We work with Ethiopian doctors, nurses, and medical students to verify our content in Amharic, Afan Oromo, and Tigrigna. EthiHealth aims to reduce misinformation, promote healthy living, and connect people to trusted health services.

Our Goals

  • ✔ Provide verified health articles in local languages.
  • ✔ Help users find nearby clinics and hospitals.
  • ✔ Offer an “Ask a Doctor” feature for basic health questions.
  • ✔ Support Ethiopia’s health awareness campaigns.
